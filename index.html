Welcome to Alpha Development Model Foundation

We are a nonprofit based in Nigeria and running a multinational education empowerment program across Africa called the Encouraging Education Program. We have our country coordinators across Africa who are supporting quality education in their various communities.

Donate Used Textbooks

Books have the power to change lives. We collect gently used textbooks and distribute them to students in rural communities who lack access to learning materials. Your donated books can spark curiosity, inspire dreams, and transform futures. Join us in making education accessible to every child.

The Data We Collect

Our aim is to collect data on out-of-school children across Africa and submit it to their respective embassies or relevant authorities. By doing so, we seek to persuade decision-makers to take action in ensuring these children return to school.

Teachers' Training

We believe globalization in education begins when teachers around the world possess the same knowledge and resources to transform their students. Through online conferences, we have been training teachers in Africa, equipping them with skills to make a lasting impact in their classrooms.

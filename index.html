Welcome to Alpha Development Model Foundation

We are a nonprofit based in Nigeria and running a multinational education empowerment program across Africa called the Encouraging Education Program. We have country coordinators across Africa who support quality education in their various communities.

Donate Used Textbooks

We collect gently used textbooks and distribute them to students in rural communities who lack access to learning materials.

The Data We Collect

We collect data on out-of-school children and submit it to their respective embassies or relevant authorities.

Teachers' Training

We train teachers in Africa, equipping them with skills to make a lasting impact in their classrooms.

Support Our Mission

Your donation helps us provide quality education for out-of-school children across Africa.
